SPEED SIMULATOR

• This application features a speedometer of a vehicle that can be interacted with various controls.

• Press the 'ENGINE' button to start/stop the engine or simply press the key I.

• Press the GAS / BRAKE pedals to change the speed of the vehicle.

• You can also use the arrow keys: ↑, ↓ or W, S to accelerate & slow down.

• Transmission mode can be toggled between Automatic & Manual when the engine is turned off, use M.

• When in 'Automatic' mode, you just have to press the GAS / BRAKE to control the speed.

• When in 'Manual' mode, you need to change the gear manually by drag & drop the tracker into the respective gear.

• The transmission map is a two way communication source in this app.

• Gear profiles and shift timing considered in this project are of Bugatti Chiron's. more profiles will be added in future.

• This is an educational project and should not be considered as commercial.
